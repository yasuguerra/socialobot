# 📊 AUDIT — Periodic Product Review

Monthly review of the product state. Owner: CPO + PM.

---

## Recommended Frequency: Monthly

## Purpose

Detect product drift (features nobody uses, metrics that don't move, accumulated UX debt) before it becomes expensive.
