# Social.Flow (socialobot) — Product Roadmap

## Roadmap Phases

### Phase 1: Core GenAI Integration & Foundation (Completed)
*   **Gemini 3.5 Upgrade**: Transition all backend logic, brand extraction, and copy optimization to `gemini-3.5-flash`.
*   **Creative Studio Sandbox**: Full integration of Imagen 3 and Veo 3.1 Pro, including progressive video extensions (+8 seconds) for narrative continuation.
*   **Multimodal Arsenal**: central asset library with automated Gemini visual analyses.
*   **Social Calendar & Scheduler**: Tailwind-based visual planning workspace.
*   **Instagram Direct Publishing**: Graph API direct container release flow.
*   **Simulated Channels**: Warnings and sandbox indicators for non-Instagram platforms.

### Phase 2: Native Channel Integrations (Upcoming)
*   **TikTok Integration**: Implement TikTok OAuth login, storage of secure creator tokens, and direct publishing of Reels/Videos via TikTok Content Publishing API.
*   **LinkedIn Integration**: Support LinkedIn OAuth 2.0 and rich-text post publishing (with images/videos) via LinkedIn Community Management API.
*   **Facebook Pages Integration**: Expand the Facebook Graph API token permissions to support publishing directly to user-connected Facebook Pages.

### Phase 3: Brand Guidelines & Advanced Audits (Long-Term)
*   **Computer Vision Brand Auditor**: Train multimodal Gemini models to evaluate generated images against the active Brand Profile's visual guidelines (logo placement, color themes, styling).
*   **Automatic Trend Catcher**: Schedule a recurring cron job for the Agentic AI Strategist to scan Google Search for local trends, automatically scheduling reactive posts.
*   **Collaborative Review Portals**: Allow agency managers to invite client users to review, approve, or reject scheduled posts before they publish.
