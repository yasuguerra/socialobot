# Social.Flow (socialobot) — Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## 1. Target Market Segments

### Segment A: B2B Social Media Agencies
*   **Persona**: Lucas (The Agency Operator)
*   **Scale**: Manages 5 to 50 brand handles for client portfolios.
*   **Key Pain Points**: High velocity of creative demands, multi-tenant asset segregation, tedious manual caption writing, and scheduling.
*   **Social.Flow Fit**: Provides multi-tenant brand isolation, fast multimodal cataloging, and quick asset reproduction via saved generative histories.

### Segment B: E-Commerce SMB Founders
*   **Persona**: Sofia (The E-commerce Owner)
*   **Scale**: Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) brands with 1-10 employees.
*   **Key Pain Points**: No dedicated marketing team, high advertising design costs, and lack of technical knowledge in scheduling or platform timing.
*   **Social.Flow Fit**: "Scan Your Site" automated profiling instantly creates a full marketing calendar without form-filling or strategic experience.

### Segment C: Visual Creators & Influencers
*   **Persona**: Carlos (The Video Creator)
*   **Scale**: Individual creators targeting Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.
*   **Key Pain Points**: Maintaining narrative continuity across consecutive posts and high costs of video rendering software.
*   **Social.Flow Fit**: Native Veo 3.1 cinematic clip generation and timeline extensions (+8 seconds) for smooth, iterative video storytelling.
