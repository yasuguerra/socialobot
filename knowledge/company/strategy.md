# Social.Flow (socialobot) — Strategy

## 1. Commercial & Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy
Our strategy is focused on targeting SMBs, D2C e-commerce founders, and marketing agencies who need to produce high volumes of high-fidelity social content with limited resources.

## 2. Core Value Proposition
Traditional tools act as database queues (e.g., Buffer, Hootsuite). Social.Flow acts as a **full-lifecycle partner**:
*   Ingests brand details via website scraping.
*   Ideates platform-specific content.
*   Synthesizes visual assets (Imagen 3) and cinematic preview clips (Veo 3.1).
*   Enables A/B testing and direct scheduling.

## 3. Growth & Lead Acquisition Channels
*   **"Scan Your Site" Demo Flow**: Prospects enter their business website URL on our landing page and instantly receive 3 custom post ideas, generated images, and optimized captions, showing immediate value.
*   **Agency Referrals**: Leverage agency operators (lucas) by offering multi-brand isolation and bulk scheduling features.
*   **Organic Content Hub**: Share daily updates and educational content about AI-driven marketing trends.
