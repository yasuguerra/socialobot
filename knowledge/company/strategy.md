# Social.Flow (Socialobot) — Strategy

## 1. Commercial & Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy
Our strategy is focused on targeting SMBs, D2C e-commerce founders, and B2B social media marketing agencies who need to produce high volumes of high-fidelity social content with limited resources.

## 2. Core Value Proposition
Traditional tools act as simple database queues (e.g., Buffer, Hootsuite) where the user must still create the content manually. Social.Flow acts as a **full-lifecycle autonomous partner**:
*   **Ingestion:** Automatically ingests brand details via website scraping (URL to brand profile).
*   **Ideation:** Brainstorms and ideates platform-specific content strategies automatically.
*   **Generation:** Synthesizes visual assets (Imagen 3) and cinematic preview clips (Veo 3.1).
*   **Optimization:** Enables A/B testing, data-driven pre-analysis, and direct scheduling.

## 3. Growth & Lead Acquisition Channels
*   **"Scan Your Site" Demo Flow**: Prospects enter their business website URL on our landing page and instantly receive 3 custom post ideas, generated images, and optimized captions. This delivers immediate "Aha!" value without signup friction.
*   **Agency Referrals**: Leverage agency operators (like our persona "Lucas") by offering multi-brand isolation, bulk scheduling, and fast generative histories that save them hundreds of hours.
*   **Organic Content Hub**: Share daily updates and educational content about AI-driven marketing trends, demonstrating our own tool's capabilities.
