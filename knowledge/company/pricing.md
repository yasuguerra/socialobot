# Social.Flow (socialobot) — Pricing Model

## SaaS Tiers

### 1. Free / Sandbox Tier
*   **Target**: Hobbyists and initial evaluations.
*   **Price**: $0
*   **Features**:
    *   Single Brand Profile (scraped from URL).
    *   3 Content Ideas per month.
    *   Access to mock social calendar (simulated channels only).
    *   Standard AI Strategist chat turns.

### 2. Creator Tier
*   **Target**: Single founders and influencers.
*   **Price**: $29 / month
*   **Features**:
    *   1 active Brand Profile.
    *   30 Imagen 3 image generations per month.
    *   Real Instagram Publishing (1 account).
    *   Simulated publishing to TikTok, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

### 3. Professional Agency Tier
*   **Target**: Multi-brand marketing managers and agencies.
*   **Price**: $99 / month
*   **Features**:
    *   Up to 10 Brand Profiles (multi-tenant isolation).
    *   Unlimited Content Ideas.
    *   150 Imagen 3 generations.
    *   10 Veo 3.1 cinematic video generations + progressive extensions.
    *   A/B Campaign Testing suite.
    *   Real Instagram Publishing (multi-account connection).
